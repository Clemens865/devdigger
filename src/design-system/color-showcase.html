Accessibility & Compliance

WCAG AAA Compliance: All color combinations meet the highest accessibility standards with contrast ratios exceeding 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.

Color-Blind Friendly: The palette uses distinct hue, saturation, and brightness values that remain distinguishable across all types of color vision deficiencies.

System Support: Includes automatic dark mode, high contrast, and reduced transparency variants.
